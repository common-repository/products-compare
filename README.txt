Effortlessly compare products in your WooCommerce store to find the best fit for your customers' needs.

== Description ==

Within your WooCommerce-powered online store, you have the ability to seamlessly compare products, allowing your customers to make informed purchasing decisions. With this feature, shoppers can easily evaluate various attributes, specifications, and prices of different products side by side. This functionality not only enhances the user experience but also empowers your customers with valuable information, ultimately leading to higher customer satisfaction and increased sales. Whether it's for electronics, fashion, or any other category, WooCommerce's intuitive product comparison tool ensures that your customers can confidently choose the best option that aligns with their preferences and requirements

== Installation ==

There is no prerequisites for installing **Products Compare**. You can follow the steps below to complete the installation.

= Automatic Installation =
1.  Go to `Plugins > Add New` screen from your WordPress website dashboard.
2.  Search for `Products Compare`.
3.  Install and activate the plugin, that's it!
